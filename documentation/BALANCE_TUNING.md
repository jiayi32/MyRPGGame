This file is the flat punch list for the P6 balance pass. Counts are regenerated by the integrity test snapshot at [src/content/__tests__/__snapshots__/integrity.test.ts.snap](../src/content/__tests__/__snapshots__/integrity.test.ts.snap) under the `sentinel audit snapshot` entry.

## Engine defaults (P2 CombatEngine)

The combat engine resolves unspecified skill effect magnitudes through a placeholder table at [src/domain/combat/defaults.ts](../src/domain/combat/defaults.ts). These are **prototype defaults**, not tuned values — they let content with `magnitude: UNSPECIFIED` run in the engine so gameplay loops can be exercised before P6 balance work begins. Every entry is a tuning candidate.

- `damage`: 1.0× strength or intellect (damageType-branched).
- `dot` / `hot`: 5% of target's max HP per tick, 4-second duration, 1-second interval.
- `heal`: 20% of target's max HP.
- `buff` / `debuff`: 10% delta on the `statTag`, 8-second duration.
- `shield`: 15% of target's max HP, 6-second duration.
- `counter`: 50% damage multiplier, 6-second duration.
- `ct_shift`: 10 flat CT.
- `execute`: triggers at ≤10% max HP.
- `lifesteal`: 30% of damage dealt.
- `status` (generic) / `summon` / `utility`: emit an `effect_stub` event so balance logs can audit untuned content.
- CT-reduction clamp: `CT_REDUCTION_CAP = 0.10` applied in [src/domain/combat/stats.ts](../src/domain/combat/stats.ts) both at base-stat resolution and when computing effective stats after buffs/debuffs. Enemy archetypes above 10% (`engineer` T3/T4) are clamped at runtime, as flagged above.
- D20 severity: fail `<10`, normal `10–17`, strong `18–19` (1.5×), critical `20` (`1 + d12 × 0.25` ∈ `[1.25, 4.0]`). Locked by [src/domain/combat/d20.ts](../src/domain/combat/d20.ts).
- Defense mitigation: `100 / (100 + 2 × DEF)` applied before resistance.
- Basic-attack fallback: units without `basicAttackSkillId` use `SYNTHETIC_BASIC_ATTACK` (1.0× physical, 40 CT, no cooldown) in [src/domain/combat/factory.ts](../src/domain/combat/factory.ts). Tune the synthetic entry when early-game TTK becomes a concern.

P6 should (a) replace the magnitude defaults with content-specific authored values wherever the placeholder diverges from the design intent, (b) re-measure the CT-reduction cap against late-game `engineer` pressure, and (c) confirm the D20 severity curve against simulated kill-time distributions.

## Snapshot integration

The integrity test emits a `sentinel audit snapshot` that counts sentinels by category path (e.g. `class.passive.magnitude`). When the snapshot changes, update this document and commit both together. A large positive delta means P1.5 or P6 added entities without tuning; a large negative delta means balance work landed — celebrate and prune this list.
